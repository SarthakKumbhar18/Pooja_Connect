Gemstone Remedies

Diamond (Heera)

Remedy: Wearing a diamond enhances love, luxury, and financial stability. It is recommended for those seeking success in relationships and creativity.

Who Should Wear: People with Venus as a strong planet in their horoscope.

How to Wear: Set in gold or platinum, worn on the ring finger of the right hand on a Friday.

Ruby (Manikya)

Remedy: Ruby is known for boosting self-confidence, leadership skills, and career success. It also improves blood circulation and vitality.

Who Should Wear: Those with a weak or afflicted Sun in their birth chart.

How to Wear: Set in gold or copper, worn on the ring finger of the right hand on a Sunday.

Sapphire (Neelam)

Remedy: Blue sapphire brings instant wealth, fame, and protection from evil influences. It also enhances intuition and mental clarity.

Who Should Wear: People with a strong Saturn (Shani) in their horoscope.

How to Wear: Set in silver or gold, worn on the middle finger of the right hand on a Saturday.

Emerald (Panna)

Remedy: Emerald is beneficial for improving communication skills, intelligence, and emotional stability. It also helps with business growth.

Who Should Wear: Those with a weak Mercury (Budh) in their birth chart.

How to Wear: Set in gold or silver, worn on the little finger of the right hand on a Wednesday.

Amethyst (Jamunia)

Remedy: This stone is known for reducing stress, anxiety, and promoting spiritual growth. It is also useful for people struggling with addictions.

Who Should Wear: Those who seek mental peace and spiritual connection.

How to Wear: Set in silver, worn on the middle finger of the right hand on a Saturday.

Topaz (Pukhraj)

Remedy: Topaz is believed to attract wealth, wisdom, and good fortune. It also helps in strengthening relationships.

Who Should Wear: People with a weak Jupiter (Guru) in their birth chart.

How to Wear: Set in gold, worn on the index finger of the right hand on a Thursday.

Garnet (Gomed)

Remedy: Garnet protects against negative energies and provides strength, courage, and stability in life. It is also known to improve health conditions.

Who Should Wear: People experiencing Rahu-related issues in their horoscope.

How to Wear: Set in silver, worn on the middle finger of the right hand on a Saturday.

Peridot

Remedy: Peridot promotes emotional balance, reduces stress, and attracts positivity in life. It also enhances self-esteem.

Who Should Wear: Those facing difficulties in self-growth and emotional stability.

How to Wear: Set in gold, worn on the little finger of the right hand on a Wednesday.
