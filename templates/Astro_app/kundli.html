What is a Kundli?

A Kundli (also known as Janam Kundali or Birth Chart) is an astrological chart that represents the planetary positions at the time of a person's birth. It is widely used in Vedic astrology to predict an individual’s future, personality, career, relationships, and more.

Importance of Kundli in Astrology

  • Personality & Character: Helps understand an individual's nature, strengths, and weaknesses.
  • Career & Finance: Provides insights into the best career paths and financial prospects.
  • Marriage Compatibility: Kundli matching (Gun Milan) is used in Hindu traditions to check compatibility for marriage.
  • Health Predictions: Indicates potential health issues and remedies.
  • Dasha & Transits: Helps in analyzing major life events based on planetary periods (Mahadasha, Antardasha).

Kundli Structure & Key Components

A Kundli is divided into 12 houses, each representing different aspects of life:

  • First House (Ascendant/Lagna): Personality, physical appearance, self-image.
  • Second House: Wealth, family, speech.
  • Third House: Siblings, communication, courage.
  • Fourth House: Home, mother, comforts.
  • Fifth House: Education, creativity, children.
  • Sixth House: Health, enemies, debts.
  • Seventh House: Marriage, partnerships.
  • Eighth House: Longevity, mysteries, sudden events.
  • Ninth House: Luck, religion, long journeys.
  • Tenth House: Career, profession, status.
  • Eleventh House: Gains, social circle, aspirations.
  • Twelfth House: Expenses, losses, spirituality.

Planets in Kundli & Their Significance

Each planet in astrology influences a person’s life based on its position in the Kundli:

  • Sun (Surya): Authority, ego, leadership.
  • Moon (Chandra): Emotions, mind, mother.
  • Mars (Mangal): Energy, aggression, courage.
  • Mercury (Budh): Intelligence, communication, business.
  • Jupiter (Guru): Wisdom, luck, education.
  • Venus (Shukra): Love, beauty, luxury.
  • Saturn (Shani): Discipline, hardship, karma.
  • Rahu: Ambition, material success, illusions.
  • Ketu: Spirituality, detachment, past karma.

Kundli Matching for Marriage (Gun Milan)

In Hindu tradition, Kundli matching is done before marriage using the Ashtakoota method. It evaluates compatibility based on 36 Gunas (points), where:

  • 18+ points: Considered a good match.
  • 24+ points: Highly compatible.
  • Below 18 points: Marriage is not recommended.

How to Read a Kundli?

  • Identify Lagna (Ascendant): Determines basic personality.
  • Check Planetary Positions: Their influence on different houses.
  • Analyze Dasha System: Predicts major life events.
  • Observe Transits (Gochar): Current planetary movements and effects.
  • Look at Yogas & Doshas: Special combinations like Raj Yoga (success) or Mangal Dosha (marital issues).

Common Doshas & Remedies

  • Mangal Dosha: Can cause marital issues, remedied by Hanuman Chalisa, fasting on Tuesdays, and Kumbh Vivah.
  • Kaal Sarp Dosha: Causes struggles in life, remedied by worshiping Lord Shiva.
  • Pitra Dosha: Family-related karma, remedied by Pitra Tarpan and Shraddha rituals.
